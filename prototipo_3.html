About Pepe

Two decades rooted in sustainable landscaping

"I began my career in the green industry at the age of 19. I've designed and installed 2,500+ jobs over my career — from small plantings to full outdoor transformations."

A VA-certified horticulturist with a B.S. in horticulture, Pepe specializes in woody plants, perennials, site planning, and turf management — guiding every client through their outdoor project with friendly, professional service.
